  3. Security Lab Recognition(SLR) Division

This Division is responsible for issuing security certificate for the successfully tested Telecom Equipment. The work will include evaluation of the test results from Security labs and issuing Security certification based on the tests performed by the designated labs. Division also assists Sr.DDG, NCCS to manage the day to day affairs of NCCS.
